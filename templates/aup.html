SiteVac provides web scraping functionality on behalf of its users. The following rules apply to all scraping activity conducted through SiteVac:

  • Users may only use SiteVac to scrape websites they own or have explicit permission to scrape.
  • Users are solely responsible for ensuring their use complies with the terms of service of any website they scrape.
  • SiteVac may not be used to scrape websites that prohibit automated access in their terms of service or robots.txt file.
  • SiteVac may not be used to scrape websites for the purpose of harvesting personal data, email addresses, or any information in violation of applicable privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA.
